- Install Selenium Ide as firefox extension :
	(type this url in firefox)
	http://release.openqa.org/selenium-ide/0.8.7/selenium-ide-0.8.7.xpi
	click on "install"
	close firefox.

- Modify in TestWordPressOnLine.html:
	line : 13 BlogURL by The url of your Blog
	line : 24 LOGIN by your login
	line : 29 PASSWORD by your password

- launch The test :
	Open firefox, go to "Tools/SeleniumIde"
	In SeleniumIde windows click on "File/Open" select the "TestWordPressOnLine.html" file.
	Press on green arrow and let starts the test.
